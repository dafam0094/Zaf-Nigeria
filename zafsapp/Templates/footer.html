From inception, since 2018, ZAF Nigeria has tremendously increased exponentially in size and structure. The Foundation has recorded by number over 1.2K Ambassadors across Nigeria and beyond – in diaspora. ZAF Nigeria has positively impacted incalculable lives through Advocacy Programs and Sensitization.

1. Sensitization on “Taming the Scourge of Rape” (#RapeIsCriminal Campaign).
2. Sensitization on “Eradicating and Eliminating the Monster of Suicide in Our Society” (#KickOutSuicide Campaign).
3. Sensitization on “Leadership & Capacity Development in Building a Sustainable Society void of Social Ills” (#FixTheMind Campaign).
4. Reaching Out to over 500 hundred Streets, Rural and Less Privileged Children (Operation Shoe and Clothe the Child)
